My Interests & Hobbies

📚 Reading Fantasy Books

I love getting completely lost in fantasy worlds — the bigger and weirder, the better. Honestly, if there are dragons, magic, or weird political intrigue, I’m in. Bonus points if it comes with a massive map in the front of the book.

🤺 Fencing

It’s half strategy, half trying not to trip over my own feet — and somehow it still feels like the coolest thing ever. Plus, who doesn’t want to secretly feel like a medieval rogue sometimes?

💻 Coding

Transforming caffeine into code and chaos into creations. I love crafting digital worlds where creativity meets logic — and sometimes, a few harmless bugs.
